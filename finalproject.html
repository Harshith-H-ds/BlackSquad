About Ecofinds

Our mission to promote sustainability

Our Story

Ecofinds was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to reduce waste and promote sustainable consumption by creating a marketplace for quality second-hand items.

Why Choose Ecofinds?

  • Reduce your carbon footprint by buying second-hand
  • Find unique items with history and character
  • Save money while shopping sustainably
  • Join a community of eco-conscious shoppers

Our Impact

Since our launch, Ecofinds community has helped divert over 5,000 items from landfills, reducing CO2 emissions by an estimated 25 tons.
